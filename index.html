I am a postdoc at the Data Systems Lab with Stratos Idreos.


The Limits of Database Indexing. I work as a part of the CrimsonDB project to identify and map the fundamentally best read/write/memory trade-offs that are possible to achieve for database indexes. See our work on the systems Monkey and Dostoevsky for details.



The Scope of Database Indexing. I am interested in extending the applicability of indexing techniques to help scientists and businesses keep up with ever-growing amounts of data. For details, see our work on Coconut (for time series similarity search) and Data Canopy (for faster statistical calculations).



Past Work. My PhD was at the IT University of Copenhagen with Philippe Bonnet, where I worked on optimising internal SSD metadata organization. See our work on GeckoFTL for details.
